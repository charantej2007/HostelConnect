Design a modern, professional, and production-ready Android mobile application UI/UX for a hostel management system called “HostelConnect”.

The application is built for hostel operations and follows an SLA-based complaint management system. The app will be published on the Google Play Store, so the UI must follow Android Material Design guidelines and be clean, scalable, and user-friendly.

The application contains three role-based portals:

1. Student Portal
2. Worker Portal
3. Administrator Portal

The design should be minimal, professional, intuitive, and attractive while maintaining usability for real-world hostel management.

Use modern Android mobile design with consistent spacing, rounded cards, soft shadows, and clear navigation.
